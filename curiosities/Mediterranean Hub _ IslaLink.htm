Mediterranean Hub

A neutral and diverse Cable Landing Station in Valencia open to all

CABLE LANDING STATION IN VALENCIA

IslaLink’s Cable Landing Station (CLS) in Valencia offers international operators and carriers a diverse, neutral landing station with connectivity into Europe via a robust fiber optic backbone.

IslaLink works in partnership with all operators and carriers to ensure the success of each project. IslaLink’s experience and on-site presence are a unique asset to those landing cables in Valencia.

Why choose Valencia over other locations?

  • Distance from the Suez Canal to Valencia = Distance from Suez Canal to French ports
  • Avoid other crowded locations to geographically diversify your risk
  • Protected cable landing area
  • Reduced level of maritime activity
  • No recorded cable incidents in over 25 years
  • Beach manholes are in place and ready to receive for cables
  • Front haul ducting to CLS is in place and ready for cables to arrive

Key Operating Advantages

  • NEUTRAL AND OPEN CABLE LANDING STATION
  • Reasonable rates for collocation space, power and electricity
  • Experienced submarine cable operator on-site (IslaLink)
  • Multiple suppliers of IP transit services at competitive prices
  • Excellent connections to European fiber optic backbone in a ring configuration
  • Easy, efficient permitting process with support of local government

A FIRST CLASS DATA CENTER

  • IslaLink ‘s Cable Landing Station (CLS) in Valencia is a first class, safe and secure technical building to house telecommunications and IT infrastructure
  • IslaLink ‘s CLS serves as a termination point for two submarine cables: BalaLink (Valencia to Mallorca) and OrVal (Valencia to Algiers and Oran).
  • CLS with the presence of more than 20 national and international Tier-1 operators and carriers.
  • Solutions for dark fiber cross-connects to easily exchange traffic with the operator networks.
  • Direct fiber interconnects to other carrier houses/data centers in Valencia.

There is immediate availability in the flily prepared technical space featuring:

  • Redundant Vca and Vcc power supplies
  • Redundant diesel generators for auxiliary power
  • Dual fire detection and extinction systems
  • Redundant air conditioning systems
  • Access security systems
  • Monitoring 24×7
